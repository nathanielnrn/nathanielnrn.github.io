About Me

I’m currently a software engineer at Netflix where I work on unifying data models by modeling with RDF graphs. Previously, I worked on tools for automating migrations, like Netflix’s recent shift to Amazon Aurora. I also interned on the Growth Engineering team to deliver backend functionality to a variety of downstream services in the name of enabling customer retention.

I graduated from Cornell University in 2024 with a Bachelors in computer science and a minor in electrical and computer engineering. There I spent time researching programming languages, computer architecture, and hardware-software interfaces through work on Calyx with the Capra group, mentored by Adrian Sampson and Rachit Nigam.

Prior to starting school I was an Air Defense Operator (“Interceptor”) and eventually a Drill Instructor in the Israeli Air Defense Command.

I hope to eventually continue my studies with a graduate degree. Please reach out if you are interested in any of my work.
